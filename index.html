I'm Tatiana...

I’m a front-end developer always on the look out for exciting new opportunities in a growing field. I feel that in order to keep up in an industry constantly changing and improving. That I have to continuously seek improvement and adjust to change.

I graduated with a bachelor degree from Full Sail University in web design and development. There I learned the essential skills needed to make me a front-end developer, and build myself the foundation to learn new things quickly and proficiently.

My strengths lie in front-end development with building content and structure, styling, adding functionality and working with various JavaScript frame-works. In the future, I see myself working towards strengthen my skills in back-end development. But until then, I’m having way too much fun designing.

I’m an imaginative and expressive individual fully inspired by the creative process. I seek stimulation for what I do through code from different aspects on my life outside of being a design nerd. I enjoy very active hobbies like long boarding and hoop dancing. As well as mentally exercising activities of writing/blogging, reading, and art.
